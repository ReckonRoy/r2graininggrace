About R2G

A Sanctuary of Solace & Remembrance

Founded in 2013 by Lebelo Grace Matila, Raining Grace Funeral Parlour (R2G) is a compassion-driven funeral service provider rooted in dignity, empathy, and personalized care.

Our name — "Raining Grace" — symbolizes divine comfort and support during grief. We are a sanctuary where families find strength, guidance, and a trusted partner to honour the legacies of their loved ones.

With a commitment to transparency, innovation, and cultural respect, we redefine the funeral experience — guiding families toward healing and remembrance.

Mission & Vision

Guided by compassion, driven by purpose — we honour every life with grace.

Our Mission

To redefine the funeral experience through Compassion, Diversity, and Innovation. We guide families toward healing and remembrance during loss, honouring every life with dignity and respect.

Our Vision

To honour legacies through inclusive funeral services, culturally respectful arrangements, empathy-driven delivery, and transparency — setting new standards of innovation in the funeral industry across Africa.

What We Offer

Our Services

Complete funeral care, from planning to aftercare — delivered with empathy and precision.

Funeral Operations

Collection, storage, planning, event management, cemetery preparation, grave bookings, digging, and building.

Transport & Logistics

Burial & Aftercare

Products & Merchandise
